Instrument Donation Services & Other Ways to Get Involved

  • Contactless pick up: submit a form on the contact page to arrange a time for Abigail to pick up your instrument(s)
  • Drop-off at a collection location: submit a form on the contact page to recieve more information about drop-off locations
  • Arrange to meet up at a neutral location: submit a form on the contact page to arrange a time to meet Abigail at a location of your choosing
  • Monetary donations: submit a form on the contact page to recieve more information about sending monetary donations
  • Want to help out? Submit a form on the contact page to express how you would like to help!
