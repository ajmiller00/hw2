Meet the Founder Abigail

I am currently a student at Tufts University, and grew up in Newton, MA. I have been playing saxophone since the fourth grade, and have loved it since the day I started. Helping kids get the chance to enjoy music as much as I do is something very near to my heart. By collecting instruments to donate, it is my hope that others will have the opportunity to share the same joy in music.
